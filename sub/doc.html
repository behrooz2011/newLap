About The Teacher

Acheiving 9 band score in IELTS test, Bruce has provided various preparation courses ranging from IELTS and TOEFL to GRE, ESL and advanced analytical writing for students aiming to get outstanding scores.He has also paved the way for hundreds of language learners from across the world to get admission from their dream universities in Canada and the US and helped dozens of ambitious candidates to go to Ivey League schools since 2010. He offers online tutorials to international students from all around the world and organizes in-person classes in London Ontario, Canada.

Mr. Bakshi's tutorial sessions are usually booked as early as two months and there are limited spots for students who are interested in attending the courses; therefore, you'll need to apply before the available seats are filled.
